About Wathaker

Faith-aware content for the questions of everyday life.

Wathaker creates reflective Islamic content and digital books about modern personal and social issues, such as phone addiction, daily pressure, and how Muslims can think about wars and conflicts they are not directly part of. The focus is awareness, behavior, and meaning, not traditional books of legal rulings about prayer, fasting, or similar topics.
